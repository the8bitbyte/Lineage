to add a site, put the url and then add '{username}' to where the username should go (example: github.com/{username}), followed by a newline for each site
github.com/{username}
www.youtube.com/@{username}
www.instagram.com/{username}
watchpeopledie.tv/@{username}
x.com/{username}
www.reddit.com/user/{username}
www.tiktok.com/@{username}
www.pinterest.com/{username}
www.tumblr.com/{username}
steamcommunity.com/id/{username}
